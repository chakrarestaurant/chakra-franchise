Your Rights & Important Advice

As a prospective franchisee, you are protected by the Franchising Code of Conduct. This section outlines your key rights and the essential advice we strongly recommend you follow.

Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD)

You must receive a complete FDD at least 14 days before signing any agreement. It contains vital, detailed information about the franchise, its financials, and your obligations.

Key Facts Sheet

You will also receive this document, which summarizes the FDD's most critical information in a clear, standard format.

14-Day Cooling-off Period

After signing the franchise agreement, you have a 14-day cooling-off period during which you can terminate the agreement.

Training, Support & Territory

We provide comprehensive training and ongoing support. You will also be granted an exclusive territory. Full details are in the FDD.

Seek Independent Advice

We strongly recommend that you seek independent legal, financial, and business advice before entering into any franchise agreement.

Conduct Your Own Due Diligence

You should carefully investigate the franchise opportunity, review all documents, and speak with your advisors before making any commitment.

Franchise Privacy Policy

Chakra Franchise Applicant Privacy Hub

An interactive guide to how we handle your information during the application process.

Welcome. This hub explains how Chakra Restaurant ("we," "us," or "our") protects the privacy of our prospective franchise applicants ("you"). We value the trust you place in us by providing your personal and financial information, and this guide details how we collect, use, share, and protect that data during the application, evaluation, and discovery process.

This policy applies exclusively to information collected from you as a prospective franchisee, including any partners or co-applicants. It is separate from our customer-facing privacy policy. The handling of your information, should you become a franchisee, will be governed by the provisions of the Franchise Agreement.

Your Information

  • Full legal name, and any former names
  • Home address, email address(es), and phone number(s)

  • Personal and professional references
  • Detailed personal and business financial statements (e.g., balance sheets, profit & loss statements)
  • Comprehensive breakdowns of your assets, liabilities, and total net worth
  • Information on your sources of funds and proof of liquid capital (e.g., bank statements, investment account statements)
  • Credit history and credit score
  • Personal and business tax returns or notices of assessment for a specified period
  • History of bankruptcy or insolvency, if any
  • Information required for criminal background checks (national and/or international)
  • Information required for credit checks (personal and business)
  • Verification of educational degrees and professional licenses
  • Civil litigation records

Our Process

How We Use Your Information

  • Application Evaluation: To assess your suitability, qualifications, business acumen, and financial capacity to own and operate a Chakra Restaurant franchise. This includes financial modeling and reviewing your proposed market or territory.
  • Verification: To conduct background checks, credit checks, and reference checks, and to otherwise verify the accuracy, truthfulness, and completeness of all information you have provided. This is a crucial step in preventing fraud and misrepresentation.
  • Communication: To maintain an open dialogue with you regarding the status of your application. This includes scheduling interviews, discovery days, or meetings; providing informational materials; answering your questions; and, if applicable, negotiating terms.
  • Legal and Compliance: To comply with all of our legal obligations, including (but not limited to) franchise disclosure laws and other applicable federal, state, and local regulations. This includes preparing and providing legally-mandated documents such as a Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD).
  • Internal Reporting: For internal analysis, planning, and reporting on our franchise development pipeline. When used for these purposes, your personally identifiable information will be removed or anonymized wherever feasible.

How We Share Your Information

We treat your information as highly confidential and do not sell it. We only share it in the following limited and necessary circumstances:

  • Internal Personnel: With our employees, legal counsel, and consultants who are directly involved in the franchise evaluation process. All such personnel are bound by strict confidentiality obligations and access your information on a "need-to-know" basis only.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: With trusted vendors who perform essential services on our behalf, such as credit reporting agencies, background check providers, legal advisors, and IT system providers. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and are strictly prohibited from using it for any other purpose.
  • Legal Authorities: If we are required to do so by law, court order, or to protect and defend the rights, property, or safety of Chakra Restaurant, our franchisees, our customers, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: If Chakra Restaurant is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to standard confidentiality agreements.
  • With Your Explicit Consent: We may share your information with other parties for purposes not listed here, but only after we have obtained your explicit, prior written consent.

Data Retention

  • If your application is successful: Your information will be transferred to your permanent franchisee file and retained for the duration of your relationship with us, governed by the terms of the Franchise Agreement.
  • If your application is unsuccessful: We will retain your information for a reasonable period necessary to comply with legal obligations, for audit purposes, or to defend against potential legal claims. Following this period, your information will be securely and permanently destroyed or anonymized.

We respect your rights and control over your information.

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to request reasonable access to the personal information we hold about you and to request that any inaccuracies be corrected.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your application and your consent for us to process your information at any time by contacting us in writing. Please note that such a withdrawal will terminate our ability to consider your application.
